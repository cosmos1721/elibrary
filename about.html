Bodhisattva Dr. B. R. Ambedkar Library

  • The Bodhisattva Dr. B.R. Ambedkar Library constructed in 1.78 lacs sqft. and spread over five story building. Established in 2008 and shifted to its new building in 2010.
  • Library has huge collection of books, journal and E-journals which cover the disciplines of Management, Computer Science, Information Technology, Biotechnology, Humanities, Social Sciences, Law, Environmental Science, Engineering, Buddhist Studies and other related areas.
  • The Library is well-equipped with modern facilities, spacious stack halls, reading halls with 2000-seating capacity, reference sections, catalogue area, digital library and is being managed by the Library staff.
  • The Central Library is a fully automated with Libsys and RFID systems.


Objectives

  • To advance and support teaching and learning activities of the university.
  • To support research activities of the university.
  • To procure, process, organize, display, facilitates access to Scholarly communications.
